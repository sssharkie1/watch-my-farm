How does WATCH MY FARM work?

  1. Record care information for all of your pets and livestock
  2. Share information securely with your farm sitter
  3. See in real time when your farm sitter completes daily chores
  4. Sit back and RELAX, knowing that you didn't forget to share a single detail about your farm!

Future Features

  • Daily farm sitter updates
  • More detailed animal profiles (including health records and photos!)
  • Garden + crop management
  • Payment integration
  • iPad/mobile app
  • Farm sitter accounts with login
